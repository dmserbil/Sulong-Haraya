About Us


SULONG HARAYA or also known us "Rhythm and Styles" is a dance group or organization inside the school of City College of Calamba. Sulong Haraya was established in the year of 2008.


Sulong Haraya


Sulong Haraya is a dance group that participates in competitions inside and outside the school also perfom when they have events in school. Sulong Haraya's type of dance is a modern dance that has a variety of genres such as hiphop, dance hall, lackings and more.This organization is not only for people who know or are good at dancing but also for people who want or willing to learn at dancing.
